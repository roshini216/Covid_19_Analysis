GUJARAT COVID 19 CASES

EMERGENCE OF COVID IN GUJARAT

The first case of the COVID-19 pandemic in the Indian state of GUJARAT was confirmed on 19 March 2020. Ever since then Gujarat has recorded 4,10,45,082 cases and11000 deaths

Gujarat recorded 14,097 Covid-19 cases, its highest one-day rise so far. The state also reported highest one-day death count of 152

Hotspots and Containment zones

Government of Gujarat declared 15 localities in four cities - Ahmedabad (8), Surat (3), Bhavnagar (2) and Vadodara (2) - as COVID-19 hotspots in the state, and decided to completely seal the areas, under the cluster containment strategy. Twenty-seven districts in Gujarat have been declared as COVID-19 'Red Zones'. These include prominent cities such as Ahmedabad, Surat, Rajkot etc. Over 31 lakh people are living in containment zones in Gujarat including 13 lakh in Ahmedabad and 8.4 lakh in Surat municipal corporation areas While Ahmedabad, Surat and Vadodraa have been placed under red zone, 17 districts are in orange zone and 13 in green zone, as per the government release.
