KERALA COVID 19 CASES

EMERGENCE OF COVID IN KERALA

In India, Kerala was the first state affected by COVID-19, and the first coronavirus case was confirmed in Thrissur district on 30 January 2020 a student, who was studying in Wuhan University and had travelled to India, testing positive for the virus. By early March the state soon had the highest number of active cases in India mainly due to a huge number of cases imported from other countries and states.
